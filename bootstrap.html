Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free to play 2d fighter with over 50+ playable characters and with each character comes with the same but different 2 weapon combinations and each character having unique moves called signatures which are unique to each character

The goal of brawlhalla

The objective of brawlhalla is very simple you have to take 3 stocks of lives from your opponent to do this you must have their character reach the out of bounds on the map on 1 of 4 areas

Why you should play brawlhalla

You should play brawlhalla not only because it is FREE and also it's because its available on all console's including mobile, windows, xbox and playstation
